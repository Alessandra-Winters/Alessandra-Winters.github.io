The Train

This is a short 4,000 word story written in a third person objective style. The main character, The Man, finds himself on a rather disturbing train with a nurse, a chef, a mob of unconcerned people, a shrunken head on a rope, and three hobos. While functioning as a horror-like story, each piece is symbolic of something in our lives.
