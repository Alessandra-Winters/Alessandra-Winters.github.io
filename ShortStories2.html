Short Stories

My minor is in Creative Writing. Through my classes I have had to write several short stories. Below I have included descriptions of each story including which narrative perspective it is written from as well as length. I am currently planning on publishing at least several (possibly all) of these stories in a personal collection in the near future. Due to this, writing samples from any and all of these stories are available upon request.

“The Train”: A short story

A 4,000 word story written in the objective third person about a man who travels through different train cars meeting strange people to discover where the train is headed and why he is on it.

“A Speech About the Economics of Albania”: A short story

A 2,000 word story written in the first person about a girl who must give a speech about the economics of Albania to a sea of spiders while standing on the edge of a cliff.

"Devil's Strawberry": A short story

A 3,000 word short story written in the second person perspective. It is about the time that you bought a magical plant from a greenhouse that’s supposed to hold all the mysteries to fix the world. And how it turns out to not be such a great house plant.
